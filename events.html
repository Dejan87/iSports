FC Yanbian Funde sports trip: Belgrade, June 15th 2018

  • June 2018

We are pleased and proud to announce that we had opportunity to host and organize sports preparations for the FC Yanbian Fund from China. So far, this was one of the most challenging task for us and impressions of our guests were confirmation of their satisfaction. All members of the club had a wonderful experience and we were honored by given trust. We are also proud of the fact that FC Yanbian is the first professional club from China who has decided to conduct preparations in Belgrade at the SC Football Association of Serbia in Stara Pazova.

- All the resources of the renowned Sports Center in Stara Pazova were available to the team from China.
- Tour of the city center was also organized.
- FC Yanbian has played 3 friendly matches:
FC Yanbian 2 : 2 FC Vozdovac (Super League of Serbia),
FC Yanbian 1 : 1 Macva Sabac (Super League of Serbia),
FC Yanbian 0 : 1 FC Krila Sovjetov (Premier League of Russia)

We thank everyone from FC Yanbian for a unique experience and we also look forward to meet again in Belgrade..

Sa zadovoljstvom i ponosom objavljujemo da smo bili u prilici da ugostimo i organizujemo sportske pripreme za FC Yanbian Funde iz Kine. Takodje smo ponosni na cinjenicu da je FC Yanbian prvi profesionalni klub iz Kine ikada koji se odlucio da sprovede pripreme u Beogradu u SC Fudbalskog Saveza Srbije u Staroj Pazovi.

- Timu iz Kine su bili na raspolaganju svi resursi renomiranog Sportskog centra u Staroj Pazovi.

- FC Yanbian je odigrao 3 prijateljske utakmice:
FC Yanbian 2:2 FK Vozdovac ( Super Liga Srbije),

iSports welcomes delegation from China

  • March 2018

iSports is very pleased and honored for being able to host sport delegation from China. During their stay, the Chinese delegation have visited FC Partizan, FC Crvena Zvezda, FC Vozdovac, FC Cukaricki, Sports Center of the Serbian Football Federation in Stara Pazova and a series of meetings were held with respective representatives from both sides. Chinese delegation was represented by: Mr. Cui Jong, the first Assistant of President and Director of the Academy of the Janbian Fund; Mr. Nui Li, Board member of the Shanghai Senhua - a member of the Premier League of China; Sun Chao, President of the Sunrise football Academy, which is also the biggest football Academy in China and has over 2,000 members in Beijing alone; Slobodan Ristić, the Director of the Sunrise football Academy and Ivan Ivović, the Founder of the iSports company. During the visit, we discussed on several topics and agreed to cooperate among different sport areas. Initial projects are planned for summer of 2018. and we expect that this is only the beginning of successful and long-lasting cooperation with our guests from China.

Veliko nam je zadovoljstvo i izuzetno smo počastvovani što je iSports bio domaćin sportske delegacije iz Kine. Kineska delegacija je obišla FK Partizan, FK Crvena Zvezda, FK Voždovac, FK Čukarički, Sportski Centar Fudbalskog Saveza Srbije u Staroj Pazovi i tom prilikom je upriličen niz sastanaka. Ispred delegacije iz Kine, sastancima su prisustvovali gospodin Cui Jong, prvi asistent predsednika i direktor akademije FK Janbian Funda, gospodin Nui Li, član borda Šangaj Senhua, inače člana Premijer lige Kine i Sun Čao, predsednik Sanrajs fudbalske akademije koja je i ujedno najveća fudbalska akademija u Kini, koja broji preko 2000 članova samo u Pekingu, direktor Sanrajs akademije Slobodan Ristić i Ivan Ivović osnivač iSports kompanije. Prilikom posete otvoreno je nekoliko tema i oblasti u sklopu kojih smo dogovorili saradnju sa našim prijateljima iz Kine, već od ovog leta očekujemo realizaciju prvih projekata koji će biti samo početak uspešne saradnje.

Elite Neon Cup 2017 Belgrade edition

  • Sep 2017

We are proud with our cooperation with YellowFields from Greece, one of the biggest sport companies in Europe, together we organized second year in row, the biggest academy tournament in Serbia, Elite Neon Cup 2017 Belgrade Edition with big success.

Elite Neon Cup has alredy been established as the most successful youth football tournament in Greece, and YellowFields, the hosting company of Elite Neon Cup, proudly announced the tournament expansion to Serbia.

Elite Neon Cup 2017: Belgrade Edition took place between 31.08-03.09. 2017 at the top Ada Facilities in Belgrade. This year tournament hosted U-16 (2002), U-14 (2004) and U-12 (2006) teams.

Each age category had eight teams initially devided into two groups of four teams. U-16 (2002): APOEL (Ciprus), Rad (Serbia), Partizan (Serbia), A.C.S. Juniorul (Romania), Altinordu (Turkey), Olimpija Ljubljana (Slovenija), Cukaricki (Serbia) and Brodarac (Serbia). U-14: (2004) Partizan (Serbia), A.C.S.Juniorul (Romania), Altinordu (Turkey), Vozdovac (Serbia), Brodarac (Serbia), Olimpija Ljubljana (Slovenia), Rad (Serbia), and Cukaricki (Serbia). U-12 (2006) Partizan (Serbia), Zemun ((Serbia), Teleoptik (Serbia), Vozdovac (Serbia), Brodarac (Serbia), APOEL (Ciprus), Rad (Serbia), and Cukaricki (Serbia).
